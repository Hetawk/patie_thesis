对论文的修改情况说明（英文）

After the thesis defense, I have fully revised the paper according to the comments and suggestions from the defense committee. The detailed revisions are as follows:

1. Measurement: The digital transformation index is a text-based proxy, so it reflects disclosure intensity rather than direct investment.
2. Identification: Treat TWFE as a benchmark only; use the modern staggered-DID results as the main interpretation.
3. Mechanisms: Financing is better read as structure adjustment, governance evidence needs caution, and knowledge spillover should remain theoretical.
4. Scope: Effects depend on estimator choice, measurement, and firm implementation capacity, and the evidence is China-specific.
5. Abstract revision: Correct the Chinese and English abstracts using the supervisor-provided translation, and make the wording clearer and more accurate.

All revisions have been completed. The revised thesis meets the academic standards for a master's degree.
