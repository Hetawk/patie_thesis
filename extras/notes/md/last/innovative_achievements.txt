B. INNOVATIVE ACHIEVEMENTS (main field)
------------------------------------------------------------------
Using China's phased A-share inclusion in the MSCI Emerging Markets Index (2018-2020) as an exogenous shock on 2,712 listed firms over 2010-2022, this thesis estimates a 0.39 log-point (about 48%) TWFE DID effect of capital-market liberalization on a validated NLP-based digital transformation index, and shows the effect attenuates under modern cohort-ATT estimators, transparently quantifying estimator sensitivity. Robustness is confirmed by event study with no pre-trends, 1,000-iteration placebo, PSM-DID, alternative outcomes, and disclosure-length normalization. A unified six-proxy mechanism test identifies financing-structure adjustment and governance strengthening as the operative channels. Effects concentrate in high-tech industries and institutionally stronger regions, consistent with an absorptive-capacity interpretation. The full Python pipeline is released for end-to-end reproducibility.
