\abstractzh{
新兴经济体资本市场自由化水平不足不利于企业融资，进而制约其转型发展。本文以中国A股分阶段纳入MSCI新兴市场指数为外生制度冲击，基于2010-2022年间2,712家上市公司数据，借助双重差分识别策略，并结合事件研究和倾向得分匹配等方法，研究新兴市场经济体的资本市场自由化能否推动企业数字化转型。

实证结果表明，纳入MSCI能够显著促进企业数字化转型提高0.39个对数点，相当于处理前水平提升约48\%。动态事件研究结果显示，纳入MSCI前的企业数字化转型不存在系统性趋势差异，纳入MSCI后的促进效应持续且具有累积特征。上述结论在安慰剂检验、分期处理诊断、替代聚类标准误、受限样本检验和替换被解释变量的检验中均成立。异质性分析发现，资本市场自由化对企业数字化转型的促进效应在高技术行业和经济较发达地区更为显著，表明吸收能力和制度互补性会影响自由化收益。机制检验显示，资本市场自由化通过缓解融资约束、强化外部监督与信息披露促进企业数字化转型，知识溢出机制未通过实证支持。

本文研究结论表明，国际金融一体化可作为新兴市场企业数字化升级的重要催化机制，因此新兴经济体应持续推进金融体制改革，着力促进企业数字化转型。

\abstracten{
The insufficient level of capital market liberalization in emerging economies hinders corporate financing, thus restricting their transformation and development. This paper takes China's phased inclusion in the MSCI Emerging Markets Index as an exogenous institutional shock. Based on data from 2,712 listed companies between 2010 and 2022, and using a difference-in-differences (DID) identification strategy, along with event studies and propensity score matching, this study investigates whether capital market liberalization in emerging market economies can promote corporate digital transformation.

Empirical results show that inclusion in MSCI significantly promotes digital transformation, increasing it by 0.39 log points, equivalent to an approximately 48\% increase relative to pre-treatment levels. Dynamic event study results indicate that before MSCI inclusion, there were no systematic trend differences in corporate digital transformation, while the promoting effect after MSCI inclusion is persistent and cumulative. The robustness of these conclusions holds across placebo tests, phase-treatment diagnostics, alternative clustering standard errors, restricted sample tests, and changes in the dependent variable. Heterogeneity analysis shows that the positive effect of capital market liberalization on corporate digital transformation is more significant in high-tech industries and more economically developed regions, indicating that absorptive capacity and institutional complementarity influence the liberalization benefits. Mechanism tests reveal that capital market liberalization promotes corporate digital transformation by alleviating financing constraints and enhancing external supervision and information disclosure, while knowledge spillover mechanisms were not empirically supported.

The conclusions of this study suggest that international financial integration can serve as an important catalytic mechanism for the digital upgrading of firms in emerging markets. Therefore, emerging economies should continue advancing financial system reforms to facilitate corporate digital transformation.
