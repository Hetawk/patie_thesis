\section{Exploratory Knowledge Spillover Proxy Test}

To align the empirical design with recent spillover studies~\cite{WangWang2026,LiuGu2026} while preserving identification discipline, this appendix reports an exploratory proxy test based on currently available merged variables. The proxy is leave-one-out peer digital transformation within industry-year cells:
\begin{equation}
\label{eq:peer_dt_appendix}
\mathrm{PeerDT}_{i,t}^{\mathrm{(ind,loo)}} = \frac{\sum_{j \in (\mathrm{ind},t),\, j \neq i} DT_{j,t}}{N_{\mathrm{ind},t} - 1}
\end{equation}
and the exploratory interaction is $\mathrm{MSCI}_{i,t} \times \mathrm{PeerDT}_{i,t}^{\mathrm{(ind,loo)}}$.
