Instructions on Thesis Defense and Archival Document Preparation
I. Before the Defense

1. After passing the blind review, complete the basic information on the first page of the non-blind review opinion form. After collecting all electronic copies, the school will submit them in groups to the defense chair for non-blind review. Once the non-blind review opinion form is returned, upload it to the graduate management system immediately. After school approval, apply for the defense in the graduate management system immediately (no later than the day before the defense) and export the Defense Application Form (the advisor signature date must match the application date).
2. If the reviewer used an electronic signature on the non-blind review opinion form, remove the electronic signature on the defense day, print one double-sided hard copy, and submit it to the defense secretary. The defense secretary will then submit it to the defense chair for handwritten signature and archiving.
3. Draft page 8 of the Degree Application Form: "Comments and Voting Results of the Thesis Defense Committee:"
   (After collecting all electronic copies, if the defense chair considers revisions necessary after the defense result is announced, you may go directly to Office 25-1818 to print a revised draft and obtain the defense chair's signature.) Print two hard copies yourself and submit them to the defense secretary on the defense day. After the result is announced, the defense chair will confirm and sign on site. For the field "Recommended degree to be conferred: XXX," fill it in as follows: MBA - Master of Business Administration (professional degree); Accounting - Master of Professional Accounting; Logistics - Master of Engineering Management; Finance - Master of Finance; Management Science and Business Administration - Master of Management; Applied Economics - Master of Economics.
4. Other preparations: Prepare N+1 printed copies of your thesis in advance (N defense committee members + yourself), double-sided (must be printed at least one day in advance). Bring them to the defense venue and submit them early to the defense secretary (mark your defense sequence number in the top-left corner of the cover for easier sorting and distribution). Prepare your defense PPT (5-8 minutes); keep content concise, focusing on your main contributions in the thesis, innovations, and practical problems solved. The expert fee for thesis defense is CNY 750 per expert, to be paid in advance by the candidate and transferred to the defense secretary before the defense (note: name and purpose). After the defense, reimbursement may be discussed with your advisor from the advisor's graduate training funds. Special reminder: neither the printed thesis submitted to the expert panel nor the PPT may include the advisor's name.
   Any place involving the advisor's name must be replaced with \*\*\*.
5. Defense time and venue: to be announced separately.
   II. During the Defense
6. Defense order: follow the sequence number in the schedule (please wait outside the defense room as early as possible to avoid leaving a bad impression on the experts. If you are late, your order will be moved back, and later candidates will move up accordingly).
7. Defense process: PPT presentation (5-8 minutes) -> expert questions (record the experts' questions and key points of your answers; after the defense, organize them into an electronic document and send it to the defense secretary) -> answer preparation (usually in groups of 3; the exact response format is decided on site by the expert panel. While others in your group are defending, prepare your own answers. After all group members complete their defense and the expert questioning, answer in sequence) -> answer expert questions -> wait outside the defense room
   -> defense result announcement (after all candidates finish, the panel enters the discussion stage, and candidates wait outside the room.

After the result is ready, the defense secretary will notify all candidates to enter the room, and the defense chair will announce the defense resolution. Everyone must be present on site.) -> group photo and photo archiving. 3. Special reminder: student presentation is 5-8 minutes; expert questioning and responses are 10-15 minutes. When time is up, the defense secretary will give a reminder, and the candidate must stop answering. Total time per person is about 20 minutes. (Please submit the printed thesis in advance to the defense secretary for distribution. Copy your presentation PPT to a USB drive and bring it on site; copy it to the defense computer before the defense begins.)
III. After the Defense

1. After completing the defense, revise and improve the thesis according to feedback from blind reviewers and defense experts, and complete the Zhejiang Sci-Tech University Graduate Thesis Revision and Finalization Form. After the advisor signs it, scan it as a PDF and upload it to the graduate management system, and complete the degree application in the system (the final deadline for degree application in the system is May 29; please complete your application by May 27 to leave 2 days for school review and committee submission). Export the relevant materials, including the degree application form. System-exported materials will automatically generate dates, so be sure to export them only after passing the defense.
